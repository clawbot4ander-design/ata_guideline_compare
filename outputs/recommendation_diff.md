## Methodology Notes

- **Grading system change:** 2015 uses ACP system (Strong/Weak, High/Moderate/Low quality). 2025 uses modified GRADE (Strong/Conditional, High/Moderate/Low/Very Low certainty) plus Good Practice Statements (GPS). "Weak" (2015) approximately maps to "Conditional" (2025), but this is a methodology change, not always a substantive strength change.
- **Staging system change:** 2015 references AJCC 7th edition; 2025 uses AJCC 8th edition. Age cutoff changed from 45 to 55 years; microscopic ETE removed from T3 definition.
- **Risk stratification change:** 2015 uses 3-tier ATA risk (low, intermediate, high). 2025 uses 4-tier ATA risk (low, low-intermediate, intermediate-high, high).
- **Pathology classification:** 2015 uses WHO 4th edition; 2025 uses WHO 5th edition (2022).
- **v1.3 upgrade:** Reference article analyses integrated directly into Layer 2 rationale text.
- **Recommendations in the 2015 nodule section (R1-R31) are excluded** from this comparison per the scope crosswalk.

## Priority Topic 1: Extent of Surgery (Lobectomy vs Total Thyroidectomy)

### [1.1] Lobectomy Threshold Expansion: <=1 cm to <=2 cm

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 35C:**
> "If surgery is chosen for patients with thyroid cancer <1 cm without extrathyroidal extension and cN0, the initial surgical procedure should be a thyroid lobectomy unless there are clear indications to remove the contralateral lobe."
> **(Strong recommendation, Moderate-quality evidence)**
> [Section B7, p.31-32]

**2025 Recommendation 15A:**
> "When resection is performed for patients with thyroid cancer <=2 cm without gross extra-thyroidal extension (cT1) and without metastases (cN0M0), the initial surgical procedure should be a thyroid lobectomy unless there are bilateral cancers or other indications to remove the contralateral lobe."
> **(Strong recommendation, Moderate certainty evidence)**
> [Section: Surgery extent, p.862]

**Change Type:** Broadened indication
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Strong -> Strong (unchanged)
**Evidence Quality Change:** Moderate -> Moderate (unchanged)

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 内文明确引用了 2015 年以后的多项大型 registry 分析作为变更依据 (p.862-863)。原文指出："Earlier ATA guidelines endorsed total thyroidectomy as the primary initial surgical treatment option for nearly all DTCs >1 cm... However, the 2015 ATA guidelines suggested lobectomy as an alternative to total thyroidectomy for cT1b-T2N0M0, low risk, unilateral PTCs... Since publication of the 2015 guidelines suggesting that thyroid lobectomy/hemi-thyroidectomy may be sufficient for low risk (cT1-2N0M0) DTC, numerous studies have been published evaluating this recommendation." 2025 guideline 进一步指出，meta-analysis 中约半数显示无 recurrence 或 survival 差异但 total thyroidectomy 并发症更高，另约半数显示 total thyroidectomy 的 recurrence rate 统计上较低。但"only one meta-analysis found improved overall survival with total thyroidectomy over lobectomy, but the benefit was confined to T2 primary tumors"。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence**: 多项 NCDB/SEER 大型 registry 分析证实 1-2 cm PTC lobectomy 后 survival 与 total thyroidectomy 相当
- **Outcome re-weighting**: 并发症风险从"可接受的代价"重新定位为"需要积极避免"
- **De-escalation philosophy**: 对低风险 DTC 的过度治疗（overtreatment）关注增加

**变动说明：**
2015 年 ATA guideline 将 lobectomy 的默认适用范围限定于 <1 cm 的 cN0 intrathyroidal PTC，而 1-4 cm 的肿瘤列为 lobectomy 与 total thyroidectomy 均可之选项（R35B）。2025 年 guideline 最重要的手术变更之一，是将 lobectomy 的默认适用上限从 <1 cm 扩大至 <=2 cm（cT1N0M0），以 strong recommendation / moderate certainty 正式确立。这一变更主要基于 2014 年以来大量 NCDB 和 SEER registry 研究的累积证据，显示在适当选择的病人中，lobectomy 与 total thyroidectomy 之间的 overall survival 和 disease-specific survival 并无显著差异，但 total thyroidectomy 的并发症风险（尤其是 hypocalcemia 和 RLN injury）远高于 lobectomy。同时，RAI remnant ablation 在低风险 DTC 中的角色被 ESTIMABL2 和 IoN 试验进一步削弱，使得"需要 total thyroidectomy 以便后续 RAI"的论点不再成立。此外，quality of life 研究一致显示 lobectomy 术后 QoL 优于 total thyroidectomy，约 70-80% 的 lobectomy 患者可避免终身甲状腺激素替代。Guideline 特别强调 shared decision-making 的重要性，并要求告知患者约 20% 可能需要 completion thyroidectomy 的可能性。

■ 关键文献：Adam et al. 2014 NCDB Analysis
- **Citation**: Adam MA et al., Ann Surg, 2014 (PMID: 25203876)
- **研究设计**: Retrospective registry analysis (NCDB), N=61,775
- **PICO**: P=PTC patients 1998-2006; I=total thyroidectomy; C=lobectomy; O=overall survival
- **主要发现**: After multivariable adjustment for tumor size, LN status, age, and other variables, overall survival was similar for total thyroidectomy vs lobectomy for tumors 1.0-4.0 cm (HR=0.96; 95% CI 0.84-1.09; P=0.54). Stratified: 1.0-2.0 cm (HR=1.05; P=0.61) and 2.1-4.0 cm (HR=0.89; P=0.21).
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 这项研究是最早也是规模最大的单一 registry 分析，直接挑战了 Bilimoria 2007 年 NCDB 分析报告的 total thyroidectomy 生存优势，证明该优势在控制更多混杂因素后消失。
- **主要限制**: Retrospective; unable to control for completeness of resection; selection bias (lobectomy patients may differ systematically from total thyroidectomy patients); limited follow-up data.
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC4532384)

■ 关键文献：Bojoga et al. 2020 Low-Risk Meta-Analysis
- **Citation**: Bojoga A et al., J Clin Med, 2020 (PMID: 32708218)
- **研究设计**: Systematic meta-analysis, 16 studies, N=175,430
- **PICO**: P=low-risk DTC; I=total thyroidectomy; C=lobectomy; O=recurrence, OS, DFS, DSS
- **主要发现**: No significant differences between lobectomy and total thyroidectomy for: recurrence (7% vs 7%; RR 1.10), OS (94.1% vs 94.4%; RR 0.99), DFS (87% vs 91%; RR 0.96), or DSS (97.2% vs 95.4%; RR 1.01).
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 这是迄今最大规模的 meta-analysis，综合 175,430 名低风险 DTC 患者的数据，确认 lobectomy 在 recurrence、overall survival、disease-free survival、disease-specific survival 四项指标上均与 total thyroidectomy 无统计学差异。
- **主要限制**: All included studies are retrospective; heterogeneity in definitions of "low risk"; most studies are PTC-dominated.
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC7408649)

■ 关键文献：Hauch et al. 2014 Complication Rates
- **Citation**: Hauch A et al., Ann Surg Oncol, 2014 (PMID: 24943236)
- **研究设计**: Retrospective registry analysis (NIS), N=62,722 procedures
- **主要发现**: Even high-volume surgeons (>99/year) had complication rate of 14.5% for total thyroidectomy vs 7.6% for lobectomy. Low-volume surgeons: 24.1% vs 11.8%.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 量化了 total thyroidectomy 的并发症"代价"，即使在最有经验的外科医师手中，total thyroidectomy 的并发症率仍接近 lobectomy 的两倍。这强化了 lobectomy 在不牺牲 oncologic outcome 的前提下显著降低 morbidity 的论点。
- **Data source**: Abstract only

**Rationale Confidence:** High (guideline 明确引用多项 registry 分析并详细讨论 rationale)

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- cT1bN0M0 PTC 患者（肿瘤 1-2 cm，无 ETE，无 LN 转移）：这是最大受益群体，从原本需要"讨论 lobectomy vs total thyroidectomy"转变为"默认 lobectomy"
- 估计占新诊断 DTC 的 30-40%

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 外科医师对 1-2 cm unilateral intrathyroidal cN0 PTC 默认推荐 lobectomy 而非 total thyroidectomy
- 减少 completion thyroidectomy 的需求（因为起始手术即已足够）
- 约 70-80% 患者可避免终身甲状腺激素替代
- 需要建立更完善的术后 Tg 监测策略（残留甲状腺组织下 Tg 判读不同）

**Impact Level:** HIGH（影响最多数新诊断 DTC 患者的初始手术决策）

**Net Effect:** 显著减少 overtreatment；降低手术并发症（hypocalcemia, RLN injury）；改善 QoL；减少医疗成本

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R35C, p.31-32] vs [2025 R15A, p.862]

|------|----------|----------|

### [1.2] Lobectomy for 2-4 cm Tumors (cT2N0M0): Preference Shift

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 35B:**
> "For patients with thyroid cancer >1 cm and <4 cm without extrathyroidal extension, and without clinical evidence of any lymph node metastases (cN0), the initial surgical procedure can be either a bilateral procedure (near-total or total thyroidectomy) or a unilateral procedure (lobectomy)."
> **(Strong recommendation, Moderate-quality evidence)**
> [Section B7, p.31-32]

**2025 Recommendation 15B:**
> "For patients with low risk, unilateral thyroid cancer >2 and <=4 cm (cT2N0M0), thyroid lobectomy may be the preferred initial surgical treatment due to significantly lower risk and side effects."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Low-moderate certainty evidence)**
> [Section: Surgery extent, p.862]

**Change Type:** Stronger directional preference (for lobectomy)
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Strong -> Conditional（强度实际降低，但方向性更倾向 lobectomy）
**Evidence Quality Change:** Moderate -> Low-moderate（降低）

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 明确指出 lobectomy 的 complication rate 显著低于 total thyroidectomy（引用 meta-analysis: transient RLN injury RR=1.7, permanent RR=1.9; transient hypocalcemia RR=10.7, permanent RR=3.2; hemorrhage RR=2.6）。对于 T2 肿瘤，guideline 承认证据存在矛盾："approximately half showing no difference in recurrence or survival but with higher complication rates for total thyroidectomy. The other approximate half demonstrate statistically significant, lower recurrence rates with total thyroidectomy compared with lobectomy alone." 但特别指出："Only one meta-analysis found improved overall survival with total thyroidectomy over lobectomy, but the benefit was confined to T2 primary tumors"（引用 Zhang 2020 meta-analysis）。然而，这项 survival 优势主要来自 Rajjoub 2018 NCDB 研究，仅限于 conventional PTC 而非 FVPTC。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence + Counterpoint**: 证据混合，约半数研究支持 lobectomy 等效性，半数显示 TT 复发更低
- **Outcome re-weighting**: 并发症代价被赋予更高权重
- **Patient-centered**: 明确要求 shared decision-making (SDM)

■ 关键文献：Rajjoub et al. 2018 (Counterpoint)
- **Citation**: Rajjoub SR et al., Surgery, 2018 (PMID: 29426618)
- **研究设计**: Retrospective registry analysis (NCDB), N=33,816
- **主要发现**: Total thyroidectomy was associated with improved survival for 2.0-3.9 cm CONVENTIONAL PTC (P=0.03) but NOT for FVPTC. For 1.0-1.9 cm tumors, no difference regardless of subtype.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 这是 2025 guideline 将 T2 recommendation 从 "strong" 降至 "conditional" 的关键原因之一 -- 证据存在矛盾，尤其是 conventional PTC 与 FVPTC 之间的差异。
- **Data source**: Abstract only

■ 关键文献：Song et al. 2019 Propensity-Matched Analysis
- **Citation**: Song E et al., Thyroid, 2019 (PMID: 30375260)
- **研究设计**: Retrospective propensity-matched analysis, N=2,345 (381 per matched group)
- **PICO**: P=1-4 cm PTC; I=lobectomy; C=total thyroidectomy; O=disease-free survival at 10 years
- **主要发现**: No significant DFS difference (HR=1.35; P=0.33). Stratified: 2-4 cm subgroup (HR=0.93; P=0.902). Surgical extent was NOT an independent factor for recurrence.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: Propensity matching 减少 selection bias 后确认 lobectomy 在 2-4 cm PTC 的 recurrence risk 并不高于 total thyroidectomy。
- **Data source**: Abstract only

**Rationale Confidence:** High（guideline 详细讨论了正反证据）

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- cT2N0M0 unilateral DTC 患者（肿瘤 2-4 cm）
- 估计占新诊断 DTC 的 15-20%

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 临床对话从"equipoise（两种手术等价）"转向"lobectomy preferred with SDM"
- 外科医师应主动告知 lobectomy 的并发症优势，以及可能需要 completion thyroidectomy 的 20-30% 可能性
- 对于 conventional PTC >2 cm，部分患者仍可能选择 total thyroidectomy（尤其老年患者或希望 RAI 的患者）

**Impact Level:** HIGH（对 T2 肿瘤手术决策产生方向性转变）

**Net Effect:** 减少 overtreatment；但需要更充分的 informed consent 与 SDM

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R35B, p.31-32] vs [2025 R15B, p.862]

## Priority Topic 2: RAI in Low-Risk DTC

### [2.1] RAI Omission in Low-Risk DTC: From Weak to Strong Recommendation

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 51A:**
> "RAI remnant ablation is not routinely recommended after thyroidectomy for ATA low-risk DTC patients."
> **(Weak recommendation, Low-quality evidence)**
> [Section B36, Table 14, p.56]

**2025 Recommendation 32A:**
> "Remnant ablation is not recommended routinely after total thyroidectomy for patients with ATA low-risk DTC."
> **(Strong recommendation, High certainty evidence)**
> [Section: RAI indications, p.885]

**Change Type:** Stronger recommendation
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Weak -> Strong
**Evidence Quality Change:** Low -> High

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 明确引用 ESTIMABL2 Phase III RCT 作为核心证据 (p.885)："A randomized Phase III trial (ESTIMABL2) evaluated 776 patients with low-risk DTC treated with total thyroidectomy... patients were randomized either to the follow-up group (no RAI) or to the ablation group (1.1 GBq following rhTSH stimulation). Of the 730 patients, the percentage of patients without an event was 95.6% in the non-RAI group compared with 95.9% in the RAI group, which met noninferiority criteria." Guideline 同时引用了 IoN trial 和 HiLo 长期随访数据。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence**: 两项独立的 Phase III RCT（ESTIMABL2 和 IoN）确认了低风险 DTC 中 RAI 的 non-inferiority/可省略性
- **New evidence**: RAI 二次恶性肿瘤风险的剂量-反应关系（Kim 2023, N=217,777）

**变动说明：**
这是 2015 到 2025 年间最重要的证据升级之一。2015 年 guideline 以 Weak recommendation / Low-quality evidence 建议低风险 DTC 不常规使用 RAI，主要依据回顾性 registry 数据和 NTCTCSG 分析。2025 年，两项独立的大型 Phase III RCT -- ESTIMABL2（法国，N=730）和 IoN（英国，N=504）-- 均证明了在低风险 DTC 中省略 RAI 的非劣性（non-inferiority），将推荐等级从 Weak/Low 升至 Strong/High。ESTIMABL2 的 3 年数据（2022 年 NEJM）和 5 年数据（2025 年 Lancet D&E）一致显示 no-RAI 策略的 event-free survival 与 RAI 组无显著差异（3 年：95.6% vs 95.9%；5 年：93.2% vs 94.8%，差异不超过 non-inferiority margin）。IoN trial 的 5 年数据同样确认了 no-ablation 策略的安全性（recurrence-free rate 97.9% vs 96.3%）。与此同时，Kim et al. 2023 年的韩国大型世代研究（N=217,777）揭示了 RAI 与二次恶性肿瘤之间的线性剂量-反应关系（HR 1.08 per increment, 从 3.7 GBq 开始显著），进一步强化了避免不必要 RAI 暴露的论点。这些 RCT 证据的出现，标志着低风险 DTC 的 RAI 决策从"缺乏充分证据的建议"转变为"有明确 Level 1 evidence 支持的强推荐"。

■ 关键文献：ESTIMABL2 Primary Results
- **Citation**: Leboulleux S et al., N Engl J Med, 2022; 386(10):923-932 (PMID: 35263518)
- **研究设计**: Phase III noninferiority RCT (open-label), N=730 (randomized from 776 enrolled)
- **PICO**: P=low-risk DTC (pT1a with sum diameters >=10mm, pT1b, N0) post-total thyroidectomy; I=no RAI; C=RAI ablation 1.1 GBq after rhTSH; O=functional, structural, and biologic events at 3 years
- **主要发现**: Event-free rate 95.6% (no RAI) vs 95.9% (RAI); difference within non-inferiority margin of -5%. No difference in structural recurrence.
- **次要发现**: QoL comparable between groups; no RAI side effects in the observation group.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 这是首个直接比较 no-RAI vs RAI 的 Phase III RCT，提供了 Level 1 evidence 证明低风险 DTC 可安全省略 RAI。
- **主要限制**: Open-label design; 3-year follow-up 对于 thyroid cancer 可能偏短；population limited to pT1aN0 (with >=10mm sum) and pT1bN0.
- **Data source**: Abstract only (NEJM subscription)

■ 关键文献：ESTIMABL2 5-Year Follow-Up
- **Citation**: Leboulleux S et al., Lancet Diabetes Endocrinol, 2025; 13(1):38-46 (PMID: 39586309)
- **研究设计**: 5-year follow-up of above RCT, N=730
- **主要发现**: Event-free survival 93.2% (no RAI) vs 94.8% (RAI); unadjusted difference -1.6% did not exceed non-inferiority margin of -5%.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 确认 3 年结果在 5 年时仍然稳定，消除了对短期随访的疑虑。
- **Data source**: Abstract only (Lancet subscription)

■ 关键文献：IoN Trial (Iodine or Not)
- **Citation**: Mallick U et al., Lancet, 2025; 406(10498):52-62 (PMID: 40543520)
- **研究设计**: Phase III noninferiority RCT, N=504, 33 UK centres
- **PICO**: P=low-risk DTC (pT1-pT3a, N0/Nx/N1a); I=no ablation; C=RAI ablation 1.1 GBq; O=5-year recurrence-free rate
- **主要发现**: 5-year recurrence-free rates: 97.9% (no ablation) vs 96.3% (ablation); absolute risk difference 0.5% (95% CI -2.2 to 3.2), meeting non-inferiority criterion.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 独立于 ESTIMABL2 的第二个 Phase III RCT 确认结论，形成了两项 Level 1 evidence 的一致性。
- **主要限制**: Included some N1a patients; UK single-country data.
- **Data source**: Abstract only (Lancet subscription)

■ 关键文献：Kim et al. 2023 RAI Dose-Response and Secondary Malignancy
- **Citation**: Kim KJ et al., J Natl Cancer Inst, 2023; 115(6):695-702 (PMID: 36821433)
- **研究设计**: Population-based cohort, N=217,777 Korean thyroid cancer patients
- **主要发现**: SPM risk increased linearly in dose-dependent manner (HR 1.08, 95% CI 1.04-1.13 per increment). Risk became significant from 3.7 GBq cumulative dose. Myeloid leukemia, salivary gland, lung cancers most elevated.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 量化了 RAI 的长期危害，强化了对低风险患者避免不必要 RAI 暴露的论点。
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC10248848)

**Rationale Confidence:** High（两项 RCT 直接引用，guideline 明确解释 rationale）

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- ATA low-risk DTC after total thyroidectomy（pT1a >=10mm, pT1b, N0/Nx）
- 估计占所有 DTC 的 40-50%

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 核医学科：低风险 DTC 不再需要常规安排 RAI 治疗
- 内分泌科：不再需要 RAI 前 thyroid hormone withdrawal 或 rhTSH preparation
- 患者：避免 RAI 相关副作用（sialadenitis 20%, menstrual irregularities 12-31%, secondary malignancy risk）
- 避免 RAI 后隔离要求和相关心理社会负担
- Follow-up 策略调整：不依赖 post-RAI WBS，改用 Tg + neck US

**Impact Level:** HIGH（影响最大比例的 DTC 患者，改变了整个 post-thyroidectomy management pathway）

**Net Effect:** 显著减少 overtreatment；消除 RAI 相关 morbidity；降低医疗成本和 radiation exposure

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R51A, p.56] vs [2025 R32A, p.885]

#### 2015 vs 2025 差异对照表

| 面向 | 2015 ATA | 2025 ATA |
|------|----------|----------|
| 低风险 RAI | Not routinely recommended (Weak/Low) | Not recommended routinely (Strong/High) |
| 核心证据 | 回顾性 registry + NTCTCSG | ESTIMABL2 + IoN RCTs |
| 证据等级 | Low quality (observational) | High certainty (2 Phase III RCTs) |
| RAI 后 follow-up | Post-therapy WBS + Tg | Tg + US only |

## Priority Topic 3: Active Surveillance for Thyroid Microcarcinoma

### [3.1] Active Surveillance as Formal Management Option

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015:** Active surveillance for thyroid microcarcinoma was mentioned only in the nodule section (R12) and as a "future research" consideration in the cancer section. No standalone DTC recommendation existed.

**2025 Recommendation 11A:**
> "Active surveillance may be offered as an appropriate management option for some patients with cT1aN0M0 PTCs. Shared clinical decision-making between the patient and clinical team regarding risks and benefits of this approach is essential."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Low certainty evidence)**
> [Section: Active surveillance, p.862]

**2025 Recommendation 11B:**
> "Ultrasound-guided percutaneous ablation may be considered as an alternative to active surveillance or resection for cT1aN0M0 PTC in selected patients."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Low certainty evidence)**

**Change Type:** Newly added (elevated from nodule-section mention to standalone DTC recommendation)
**Recommendation Strength Change:** N/A (no prior DTC recommendation) -> Conditional
**Evidence Quality Change:** N/A -> Low

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 引用了 systematic review 的结果 (p.862)："A systematic review published by a subgroup of this guidelines task force reviewed the published literature regarding active surveillance versus immediate resection and found low-certainty evidence that in adults with small, low-risk DTC, active surveillance and immediate resection are associated with similar, low risk of all-cause or cancer-specific mortality, distant metastasis, and recurrence after thyroidectomy." 同时指出："In patients managed with active surveillance, rates of tumor growth were low. Data on harms were limited, but temporary vocal fold paralysis and hypoparathyroidism were complications from thyroid surgery."

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence**: 日本 Kuma Hospital 30 年经验（N=5,646）和韩国 KoMPASS 前瞻性研究（N=755）
- **De-escalation philosophy**: 对 papillary thyroid microcarcinoma 过度治疗的关注
- **Patient-centered**: 提供非手术路径的选择

**变动说明：**
Active surveillance（AS）的引入是 2025 年 guideline 最具变革性的概念之一。虽然 2015 年 guideline 在结节评估章节（R12）和 DTC 讨论中提及了 AS 的可能性，但并未将其纳入正式 DTC recommendation。2025 年将 AS 正式确立为 cT1aN0M0 PTC 的"appropriate management option"（R11），这一变更主要基于三方面证据的累积：(1) Kuma Hospital 30 年 AS 经验（Miyauchi & Ito 2023, N=5,646），显示 AS 患者中无一人死于甲状腺癌，10 年肿瘤增大率仅 4.7%，淋巴结转移率 1.0%；(2) 韩国 KoMPASS 多中心前瞻性研究（Lee et al. 2022, N=755），验证了 AS 在东亚以外人群中的安全性；(3) MSKCC 团队（Tuttle 等）在西方人群中的 AS 实践经验。2025 guideline 同时新增了 percutaneous ablation（热消融）作为 AS 或手术的替代选项（R11B），反映了这一新技术在 PTMC 管理中的兴起。监测方案（R12-14）也首次标准化：q6 month US x 1-2 年，之后 annually；不常规使用 Tg 监测（因完整甲状腺下 Tg 不可靠）；定义了需要手术干预的 triggers（肿瘤明显增大、新发 LN 转移等）。

■ 关键文献：Miyauchi & Ito 2023 Kuma Hospital 30-Year Data
- **Citation**: Miyauchi A, Ito Y et al., Thyroid, 2023; 33(7):817-825 (PMID: 37166389)
- **研究设计**: Retrospective cohort, N=5,646 (3,222 AS, 2,424 immediate surgery)
- **PICO**: P=PTMC (<=1 cm); I=active surveillance; C=immediate surgery; O=tumor progression, LN metastasis, disease-specific mortality
- **主要发现**: 10-year tumor enlargement rate 4.7%; 20-year enlargement rate 6.6%. LN metastasis 1.0% at 10 years, 1.6% at 20 years. ZERO disease-specific deaths over up to 30 years in either group.
- **次要发现**: AS required significantly fewer surgeries overall; patients who underwent conversion surgery after AS had outcomes identical to immediate surgery patients.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 这是全球最大且随访最长的 AS 世代研究，30 年零疾病特异性死亡的数据为 AS 的安全性提供了最终证据。
- **主要限制**: Single institution; retrospective; predominantly Japanese population; potential selection bias.
- **Data source**: Full text available via PMC

■ 关键文献：Lee et al. 2022 KoMPASS
- **Citation**: Lee EK et al., Thyroid, 2022; 32(11):1328-1336 (PMID: 36205563)
- **研究设计**: Multicenter prospective cohort, N=755 (AS) vs 422 (surgery)
- **PICO**: P=cT1aN0M0 PTC; I=active surveillance; C=immediate surgery; O=progression rate
- **主要发现**: 5-year progression rate 14.2% (higher than Japanese data). Risk factors for progression: age <30 (OR 2.86), male sex (OR 2.48), tumor >=6mm (OR 1.89). ZERO distant metastases during AS.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 首个大规模韩国多中心前瞻性 AS 数据，验证了 AS 在非日本人群中的安全性，同时识别了人群特异性风险因素。
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC9700369)

**Rationale Confidence:** High（guideline 明确引用 systematic review 和多项世代研究）

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- cT1aN0M0 PTC 患者（肿瘤 <=1 cm，无 ETE，无 LN 转移）
- 估计占新诊断甲状腺癌的 40-50%（得益于 US 筛查的普及，microcarcinoma 发现率极高）

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 内分泌科：需要建立 AS program 的基础设施（标准化 US 监测方案、patient education、心理支持）
- 外科：部分患者不再需要转介手术
- 影像科：AS 患者需要 q6 month -> annual US 监测
- 患者：获得"不手术"的正式选项，避免手术并发症和"癌症"诊断的心理负担

**Impact Level:** HIGH（创建了全新的非手术管理路径）

**Net Effect:** 最大程度减少 overtreatment；避免手术并发症；减少医疗成本

**Citation Anchors:** [2015: no DTC recommendation, R12 in nodule section] vs [2025 R11, p.862]

## Priority Topic 4: Prophylactic Central Neck Dissection

### [4.1] Prophylactic CND for T1-T2 cN0: From "Appropriate" to "Should Not"

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 36C:**
> "Thyroidectomy without prophylactic central neck dissection is appropriate for small (T1 or T2), noninvasive, clinically node-negative papillary thyroid carcinoma."
> **(Weak recommendation, Low-quality evidence)**
> [Section B8, p.34]

**2025 Recommendation 19A:**
> "Prophylactic CND should not be performed for most patients with T1-T2 cN0 PTC."
> **(Strong recommendation, Moderate certainty evidence)**
> [Section: Lymph node dissection, p.867]

**Change Type:** Stronger recommendation (against pCND)
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Weak -> Strong
**Evidence Quality Change:** Low -> Moderate

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 在 R19 rationale 中详细讨论了 pCND 的 risk-benefit tradeoff。虽然 pCND 可以发现隐匿性淋巴结转移（upstage from cN0 to pN1a），但多项 meta-analyses 一致显示：pCND doubles transient hypocalcemia（OR 2.23-2.56），doubles permanent hypocalcemia（OR 1.93-4.24），增加 transient RLN injury，但 recurrence 的绝对降低幅度非常小（NNT=31）。关键是，microscopic pN1a 的临床意义远低于 macroscopic cN1a，pCND 发现的微小淋巴结转移可能导致 unnecessary upstaging 和过度使用 RAI。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence**: 多项 post-2015 meta-analyses 量化了 pCND 的 risk-benefit tradeoff
- **Reinterpretation**: microscopic N1a vs macroscopic N1a 的预后差异被重新认识
- **De-escalation philosophy**: 减少 morbidity-generating procedures with marginal benefit

**变动说明：**
2015 年 guideline 用双重否定的措辞表达了对不做 pCND 的接受 -- "thyroidectomy without prophylactic central neck dissection is appropriate"（R36C, Weak/Low）。2025 年将措辞改为明确的否定 -- "prophylactic CND should not be performed"（R19A, Strong/Moderate）。这不仅仅是措辞差异，而是从"不做也可以"转变为"不应该做"的根本性态度变化。驱动这一变化的核心证据来自 2015 年以来发表的多项大型 meta-analyses，特别是 Chen et al. 2018（N=18,376）显示 pCND doubled both transient（OR 2.23）and permanent hypocalcemia（OR 2.22），Wang et al. 2013 的 NNT=31 计算（需要对 31 名患者进行 pCND 才能避免 1 例复发），以及最新的 Wang et al. 2023 meta-analysis 显示 permanent hypocalcemia OR 高达 4.24。同时，2025 年 4-tier risk stratification 将 microscopic N1a 从"intermediate risk"中分离出来，放入"low-intermediate"类别，进一步降低了 pCND 发现的隐匿性微小淋巴结转移的临床意义。这一变化还与 lobectomy 适用范围的扩大相辅相成 -- 如果大多数 cT1-T2 N0 肿瘤现在以 lobectomy 为默认术式，那么同侧 CND 仅在有临床证据时才进行。

■ 关键文献：Chen et al. 2018 pCND Meta-Analysis
- **Citation**: Chen L et al., World J Surg, 2018; 42(9):2846-2857 (PMID: 29488066)
- **研究设计**: Systematic review and meta-analysis, 23 studies, N=18,376
- **主要发现**: pCND significantly reduced LRR (OR 0.65; 95% CI 0.48-0.88) BUT significantly increased transient RLN injury (OR 2.03), transient hypocalcemia (OR 2.23; 95% CI 1.84-2.70), and PERMANENT hypocalcemia (OR 2.22; 95% CI 1.58-3.13).
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 最大规模的 pCND meta-analysis 量化了"modest recurrence reduction vs doubled morbidity"的 tradeoff，构成 2025 recommendation 的核心证据。
- **Data source**: Abstract only

■ 关键文献：Wang TS et al. 2013 NNT Analysis
- **Citation**: Wang TS et al., Ann Surg Oncol, 2013; 20(11):3477-83 (PMID: 23846784)
- **研究设计**: Meta-analysis, 11 studies, N=2,318
- **主要发现**: Overall recurrence 7.6% (TT alone) vs 4.7% (TT+pCND); RR 0.59 (95% CI 0.33-1.07) -- trend but NOT statistically significant. NNT=31.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: CI crosses 1.0，表明 recurrence benefit 未达统计显著性；NNT=31 显示 absolute benefit 极小。
- **主要限制**: CI crosses 1.0; smaller sample than Chen 2018.
- **Data source**: Abstract only

**Rationale Confidence:** High

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- 所有 T1-T2 cN0 PTC 患者接受 thyroidectomy（或 lobectomy）
- 估计占 PTC 手术的 70-80%

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 外科医师不再常规添加 central neck dissection 于 T1-T2 cN0 PTC 手术
- 显著降低术后 transient hypocalcemia（从约 26% 降至约 10%）和 permanent hypocalcemia
- 减少因 incidental pN1a 发现导致的 unnecessary RAI upstaging
- 仅在术中发现可疑淋巴结时进行 therapeutic CND

**Impact Level:** HIGH（影响大多数 PTC 手术的手术范围决策）

**Net Effect:** 显著减少手术 morbidity；减少 unnecessary upstaging 和 over-treatment with RAI

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R36C, p.34] vs [2025 R19A, p.867]

## Priority Topic 5: Risk Stratification System

### [5.1] From 3-Tier to 4-Tier ATA Risk Classification

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 48:**
> Three-tier risk stratification system: Low / Intermediate / High
> **(Strong recommendation, Moderate-quality evidence)**
> [Section B28-B31, p.39-48]

**2025 Recommendation 28:**
> "The 2025 ATA Risk Stratification System, which evaluates the histopathologic features of the tumor and number of cervical lymph nodes... is recommended to determine the risk of structural disease persistence/recurrence."
> Four-tier system: Low / Low-Intermediate / Intermediate-High / High
> **(Strong Recommendation, Moderate certainty evidence)**
> [Section: Risk stratification, p.874]

**Change Type:** Replaced framework
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Strong -> Strong (unchanged)
**Evidence Quality Change:** Moderate -> Moderate (unchanged)

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 明确指出分拆"intermediate"类别的原因 (p.874)："Previous ATA guidelines recommended a three-tiered categorical system stratifying patients as low, intermediate, or high risk... The current system identifies specific features of DTC that should be considered when determining the overall risk of future structural persistence/recurrence for an individual patient. The higher risk of recurrence is frequently dependent on co-existing factors rather than a single factor." 2015 年的 "intermediate" 类别涵盖了风险差异极大的群体 -- 从 microscopic N1a（recurrence ~5%）到 aggressive histology with macroscopic N1a（recurrence ~20-30%）。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **Reinterpretation**: 2015 "intermediate" 类别的 heterogeneity 被多项后续研究证实
- **Methodology change**: AJCC 8th edition 将 microscopic ETE 移出 T3 definition
- **New evidence**: Multiple studies differentiating microscopic vs macroscopic N1a outcomes

**变动说明：**
2015 年 ATA 三层风险分层系统（Low / Intermediate / High）在临床实践中表现出"intermediate"类别异质性过大的问题 -- 该类别涵盖了从 microscopic N1a（复发率约 5%）到 extensive vascular invasion（复发率 15-30%）到 aggressive histology（复发率更高）的广泛范围。2025 年将 intermediate 分拆为 low-intermediate 和 intermediate-high，创建了四层系统。这一改变的核心依据包括：(1) AJCC 8th edition 将 microscopic ETE 从 T3 定义中移除，使得原本被归为 T3/intermediate 的大量小肿瘤伴 microscopic ETE 降级为 T1-T2；(2) 多项研究区分了 microscopic vs macroscopic N1a 的截然不同预后；(3) 新增因素如 vascular invasion in PTC、multifocality pattern、LNR 等的预后价值被进一步验证。四层系统使得 RAI 使用、TSH suppression 目标、surveillance intensity 等后续决策能够更精准地匹配个体风险。

**Rationale Confidence:** High

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- 原 2015 "intermediate risk" 患者（估计占 DTC 的 30-40%）
- 特别是 microscopic N1a 患者（被降级至 low-intermediate）和 aggressive histology 患者（被升级至 intermediate-high）

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 内分泌科/外科：需要重新学习和应用新的四层分类系统
- RAI 决策更精准：low-intermediate 更倾向省略 RAI；intermediate-high 更倾向使用 RAI
- TSH suppression 目标个体化：low-intermediate 目标为 normal range；intermediate-high 可能需要 subnormal range
- 需要更详细的病理报告以支持四层分类

**Impact Level:** HIGH（影响分层、RAI 决策、TSH 目标、surveillance intensity 的整个决策链）

**Net Effect:** 减少 intermediate 类别的 "one-size-fits-all" 问题；同时可能减少 overtreatment（low-intermediate 组）和 undertreatment（intermediate-high 组）

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R48, p.39-48] vs [2025 R28, p.874]

## Priority Topic 6: TSH Suppression Targets

### [6.1] TSH Suppression De-escalation

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 70 (cross-referenced):**
> High-risk: TSH <0.1 mU/L recommended; Low-risk excellent response: TSH 0.5-2 mU/L
> **(Strong/Weak, Moderate/Low-quality evidence)**
> [Section C1, p.63-64]

**2025 Recommendation 45:**
> "Individualization of decisions to initiate TSH suppression to below the reference range is recommended based on potential benefits and risks; recognizing that patients with high-risk disease may be more likely to benefit from a TSH in the subnormal range than those with low-risk disease."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Low certainty evidence)**
> [Section: TSH suppression, p.896]

**2025 Recommendation 46A:**
> "Long-term TSH suppression is not suggested for patients with low- or intermediate-risk disease who have no evidence of biochemical or structural recurrence."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Low certainty evidence)**

**Change Type:** Weaker recommendation (for suppression); stronger against unnecessary suppression
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Strong -> Conditional (for high-risk); Weak -> more explicit (against suppression in low-risk)
**Evidence Quality Change:** Moderate/Low -> Low (uncertainty acknowledged more explicitly)

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 用大量篇幅讨论 TSH suppression 的矛盾证据 (p.896-897)。引用了 NTCTCSG 2015 年的关键研究："among 3238 patients with DTC, there was improved overall survival and disease-free survival across all stages for mean TSH scores in the moderate suppression range (i.e., subnormal to normal TSH) and no further improvement for any stage with TSH levels averaging in the undetectable subnormal range." 同时引用了对 lobectomy 后 TSH suppression 的多项研究，一致显示 TSH 水平与 recurrence 无显著关联。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence**: NTCTCSG registry update（N=3,238）showing no incremental benefit from aggressive suppression
- **New evidence**: Cardiovascular/bone morbidity 的 meta-analyses（AF risk RR 1.52; BMD loss in postmenopausal women）
- **Outcome re-weighting**: TSH suppression 的"代价"（AF、骨质疏松、QoL 下降）被重新权衡

■ 关键文献：Carhill et al. 2015 NTCTCSG Update
- **Citation**: Carhill AA et al., J Clin Endocrinol Metab, 2015; 100(9):3270-3279 (PMID: 26171797)
- **研究设计**: Prospective registry analysis, N=4,941 (used 3,238 with complete data), median 6-year follow-up
- **主要发现**: Moderate TSH suppression (subnormal-normal range) was associated with improved OS and DFS across all stages. NO additional survival benefit from aggressive suppression (undetectable-subnormal), even in patients who developed distant metastatic disease.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 直接证明"undetectable TSH"相比"subnormal TSH"没有额外生存获益，但增加心血管和骨骼风险。
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC5393522)

■ 关键文献：Yang et al. 2022 AF Risk Meta-Analysis
- **Citation**: Yang X et al., Front Endocrinol, 2022; 13:991876 (PMID: 36619576)
- **研究设计**: Meta-analysis, 14 studies
- **主要发现**: TSH suppression therapy increases AF risk (RR 1.52, 95% CI 1.28-1.81). Also associated with higher heart rate and blood pressure.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 量化了 TSH suppression 的心血管危害，特别是心房颤动风险增加 52%。
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC9814721)

■ 关键文献：Ku et al. 2021 BMD Meta-Analysis
- **Citation**: Ku EJ et al., J Clin Endocrinol Metab, 2021; 106(12):3655-3667 (PMID: 34302730)
- **研究设计**: Meta-analysis, 17 studies, 739 patients, 1085 controls
- **主要发现**: Postmenopausal women on TSH suppression had significantly decreased lumbar spine BMD (-0.03; p<0.05). Premenopausal women and men showed no decrease.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 证明 TSH suppression 对停经后女性骨密度的不良影响，支持在低风险患者中避免不必要的 TSH suppression。
- **Data source**: Abstract only

**Rationale Confidence:** High

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- Low-risk DTC patients（特别是 lobectomy 后的 70-80% 不需要甲状腺激素补充的患者）
- 停经后女性（BMD 风险）和老年患者（AF 风险）
- High-risk 患者也受影响：从"undetectable TSH"转向"subnormal TSH"

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 内分泌科：低风险/中低风险患者 TSH 目标为 normal reference range，不再追求 suppression
- Lobectomy 后约 70-80% 患者可不需要甲状腺激素补充
- 高风险患者：从"aggressive suppression"（TSH <0.1）转向"moderate suppression"（subnormal TSH）
- 减少对甲状腺激素剂量的频繁调整和 TSH 监测频率
- 需要与患者讨论 TSH suppression 的 risk-benefit（SDM）

**Impact Level:** HIGH（影响所有 DTC 患者的长期甲状腺激素管理策略）

**Net Effect:** 减少 overtreatment；降低心血管和骨质疏松 morbidity；改善 QoL

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R70, p.63-64] vs [2025 R45-R46, p.896-897]

## Priority Topic 7: Surveillance De-escalation and "Complete Remission"

### [7.1] Defining Endpoints and Stopping Surveillance

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendations 62-65:**
> Ongoing Tg q6-12 months; periodic neck US; no defined stopping point for surveillance. Follow-up was essentially lifelong.
> [Section C5-C8, p.59-62]

**2025 Recommendation 48:**
> 1. "For patients with low-risk DTC treated with total thyroidectomy and RAI and a sustained excellent response 5-8 years after initial therapy, routine ultrasound can be discontinued..."
> 2. "Patients with low-risk DTC treated with total thyroidectomy and RAI and sustained excellent response for 10-15 years do not require continued routine biochemical monitoring for thyroid cancer and should be considered to have achieved a complete remission."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Low certainty; Good Practice Statement)**
> [Section: Monitoring de-escalation, p.900; Table 11]

**Change Type:** Newly added (complete remission concept and defined stopping rules)
**Recommendation Strength Change:** N/A -> Conditional / GPS
**Evidence Quality Change:** N/A -> Low

#### Layer 2: Rationale

**Guideline's Own Explanation:**
2025 guideline 引入了 "complete remission" 这一肿瘤学概念 (p.900)："'Complete remission' is the term used in oncology to define the disappearance of all signs of cancer in response to treatment... Identifying patients to be in complete remission offers psychological, financial, and medical value to both patients and clinicians." 引用了多项长期 follow-up 研究显示：低风险 DTC excellent response 患者的 structural recurrence 率在 5 年后 <1.4%，且 85% 的 recurrence 发生在前 5 年。

**Evidence Driver:**
- **New evidence**: 多项 >1000 人、>10 年 follow-up 世代研究
- **New evidence**: False-positive US rate 在长期 follow-up 中超过真正 recurrence rate
- **Implementation feedback**: 终身 follow-up 的经济、心理和医疗资源负担
- **Patient-centered**: 减少不必要的 anxiety 和 healthcare utilization

■ 关键文献：Seejore et al. 2021
- **Citation**: Seejore K et al., Clin Endocrinol, 2021; 96(3):395-401 (PMID: 34185343)
- **研究设计**: Retrospective cohort, N=756, median follow-up 11.2 years
- **PICO**: P=low-risk DTC after TT + RAI with excellent response; I=continued monitoring; O=structural recurrence
- **主要发现**: Radiological recurrence in only 15/756 (2.0%); 85% of recurrences identifiable within 5 years. All recurrences preceded by raised Tg or TgAb levels.
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 证明 5 年后 excellent response 的低风险 PTC 患者 recurrence 极少，且 biochemical markers 先于 imaging 发现 recurrence，支持停止常规 US 监测。
- **Data source**: Abstract only

■ 关键文献：Tran et al. 2024 Competing Risk Analysis
- **Citation**: Tran TV et al., Thyroid, 2024; 34(2):215-224 (PMID: 38149602)
- **研究设计**: SEER population-based analysis, N=51,854, median 8.8-year follow-up
- **主要发现**: Among low-risk DTC patients, thyroid cancer accounted for only 4.3% of deaths (148/3,467). 20-year thyroid cancer-specific mortality was only 0.6%. Most deaths from other malignancies (29.7%) and CVD (26.3%).
- **为什么支持此 recommendation 变更**: 竞争风险分析证明低风险 DTC 患者的死亡风险主要来自非甲状腺原因，过度监测的边际获益极低。
- **Data source**: Full text (PMC10884550)

**Rationale Confidence:** High

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:**
- 数十万现有低风险 DTC 幸存者（sustained excellent response >5 年者）
- 这是影响患者人数最多的单一变化

**What Changes in Practice:**
- 内分泌科：低风险 excellent response 5-8 年后可停止常规颈部 US
- 10-15 年后可停止 Tg biochemical monitoring，宣布"complete remission"
- 基层医疗/家庭医学：可接回低风险 DTC 长期存活者的 routine care
- 患者：从"终身癌症追踪"转变为"有终点的追踪 -> 完全缓解 -> 出院"
- 心理获益：减少"cancer survivor identity"的长期心理负担

**Impact Level:** HIGH（影响现存的数十万 DTC 幸存者）

**Net Effect:** 减少不必要的医疗利用和成本；减少 false-positive imaging 导致的侵入性检查；改善患者心理健康

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R62-65, p.59-62] vs [2025 R48, p.900; Table 11]

**Evidence Quality Change:** Moderate -> Low-moderate

#### Layer 2: Rationale

2025 guideline 指出大多数 incidentally discovered cancers are low-risk cancers adequately treated with lobectomy alone. 同时 AJCC 8th edition 移除 microscopic ETE from T3 definition 消除了许多原本需要 completion thyroidectomy 的 triggers。原文："most cytologically indeterminate cases that prove to be malignant on final histology are low-risk cancers adequately treated with lobectomy alone"（p.865）。Decision-making 现在需要综合考虑 postoperative risk assessment、RLN 功能状态、和 patient preference。

**Evidence Driver:** Reinterpretation (AJCC 8th changes) + De-escalation + Patient-centered
**Rationale Confidence:** High

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:** Incidental DTC after lobectomy for indeterminate nodule (approximately 15-25% of lobectomies)
**What Changes in Practice:** Fewer patients referred for second surgery; more patients managed with lobectomy alone
**Impact Level:** Moderate (affects decision pathway but within existing framework)
**Net Effect:** Reduces morbidity from unnecessary second surgery

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R38A, p.35] vs [2025 R16A, p.865]

### [9] Tg Monitoring After Lobectomy: Reversed Recommendation

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 64:**
> "Periodic serum Tg should be considered during follow-up of patients who have undergone less than a total thyroidectomy."
> **(Strong recommendation, Low-quality evidence)**

**2025 Recommendation 47D:**
> "Measurement of serum Tg on thyroid hormone in patients after lobectomy during initial follow-up is not recommended routinely."
> **(Conditional recommendation, Very low certainty evidence)**

**Change Type:** Reversed recommendation
**Recommendation Strength Change:** Strong -> Conditional (direction reversed)
**Evidence Quality Change:** Low -> Very low

#### Layer 2: Rationale

2025 guideline 明确指出："serum Tg has not been shown to predict recurrence in patients with DTC after thyroid lobectomy, but this is not well studied to date"（p.899）。残留正常甲状腺组织下的 Tg 基线水平使得 Tg 监测在 lobectomy 后无法有效区分 normal thyroid tissue vs residual/recurrent cancer。Tg 的 variability 在有正常甲状腺组织存在时过大，使其作为 tumor marker 的价值大打折扣。

**Evidence Driver:** Reinterpretation (Tg unreliable with intact contralateral lobe) + Implementation feedback
**Rationale Confidence:** Moderate (guideline acknowledges limited evidence)

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:** Growing population of lobectomy patients (increasing due to expanded lobectomy indications)
**What Changes in Practice:** No routine serial Tg measurements after lobectomy; rely on US surveillance instead
**Impact Level:** Moderate
**Net Effect:** Reduces unnecessary biochemical testing; reduces patient anxiety from fluctuating Tg levels

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R64, p.61] vs [2025 R47D, p.898]

### [11] rhTSH for RAI Preparation: From "Alternative" to "Preferred"

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 54:**
> "rhTSH-mediated preparation can be used as an alternative to thyroid hormone withdrawal..."
> **(Strong recommendation, High-quality evidence)**

**2025 Recommendation 34A:**
> "In patients with DTC in whom RAI remnant ablation or adjuvant therapy is planned, preparation with rhTSH stimulation is preferred over thyroid hormone withdrawal."

**Rationale Confidence:** High

#### Layer 3: Clinical Impact

**Who Is Most Affected:** All DTC patients receiving RAI
**What Changes in Practice:** rhTSH becomes default preparation method; thyroid hormone withdrawal reserved for special circumstances
**Impact Level:** Moderate
**Net Effect:** Improved QoL during RAI preparation; reduced hypothyroid symptoms

**Citation Anchors:** [2015 R54, p.57] vs [2025 R34A, p.888]

### [14] Targeted Therapy for RAIR DTC: Replaced Framework

#### Layer 1: Observation

**2015 Recommendation 96:**

**2025 Recommendations 61-72:**

**Impact Level:** HIGH (for RAIR subgroup, which is small but has high morbidity)
**Net Effect:** Improved response rates; better tolerability; precision medicine approach

## Compounding Effects Analysis

### De-escalation Cascade

2025 年 guideline 的变化不是孤立的——它们形成了一个相互强化的 de-escalation cascade：

1. **更多 lobectomy**（R15: <=2 cm 默认 lobectomy）-> 2. **更少 completion thyroidectomy**（R16: "may be considered" vs "should be offered"）-> 3. **更少 RAI**（R32: Strong/High against RAI in low-risk）-> 4. **更少 TSH suppression**（R45-46: not suggested for low/intermediate without recurrence）-> 5. **更少 surveillance**（R48: defined stopping rules at 5-8 years and 10-15 years）

对于一个典型的 1.5 cm cT1bN0M0 PTC 患者：
- **2015 pathway**: total thyroidectomy -> possible RAI -> TSH suppression (0.5-2.0) -> lifelong Tg + US monitoring
- **2025 pathway**: lobectomy -> no RAI -> normal TSH -> limited US monitoring 5-8 years -> complete remission at 10-15 years

This represents a transformative reduction in treatment intensity that affects the majority of newly diagnosed DTC patients.
